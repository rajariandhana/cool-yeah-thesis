# Cool Yeah Thesis
Insitut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Indonesia requires their undergraduate student to write a thesis in order to graduate. As an International Undergraduate Program (IUP) student of ITS I partake in their Double Degree (DD) program where I studied for 5 semesters in ITS (August 2022 - December 2024) and 3 semesters in The University of Queensland (UQ) Australia (February 2025 - June 2026) as the partner university I chose. This thesis is converted from the final project I have completed at UQ's Design Computing Studio 3 - Build (DECO3801) course. This repository contains all the necessary files for my thesis writing.
